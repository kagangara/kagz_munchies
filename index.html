About our hotel

Welcome to our humble hotel, a hidden gem nestled in the vibrant heart of Ubuntu Village. As the proprietor, I take great pride in welcoming each guest with authentic Kenyan warmth and hospitality. Our cozy space is a sanctuary for locals and travelers alike, offering a taste of our rich culture through every sip and savory bite. Our menu is a celebration of Kenya's diverse culinary heritage, featuring the finest selection of freshly brewed Kenyan coffee, aromatic chai, and delectable samosas bursting with flavorful fillings. We've sourced our coffee beans from the lush highlands of Kenya, ensuring that every cup tells the story of our country's renowned coffee-growing tradition. Our hotel is a meeting point for friends, families, and kindred spirits. Whether you're seeking a moment of tranquility to savor your coffee, engage in lively conversation, or simply soak in the vibrant atmosphere, our doors are always open to you. With the melody of local music in the background and the welcoming smiles of our team, every visit to our café is an embrace of Kenyan warmth and camaraderie. Join us as we invite you to experience the heart and soul of Kenya, one sip at a time, in our cherished little café. Karibu sana! (You are very welcome!)

Feel free to visit our hotel to have a taste of our delicacies. KARIBU!!!
